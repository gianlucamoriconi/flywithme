AEYE Health
Ver más

AEYE Health has created a simple, AI-driven testing system for an array of eye diseases including diabetic retinopathy, glaucoma and AMD without requiring the knowledge or training of an ophthalmologist. All people with diabetes, some 35 million people in the US alone, are at risk of diabetic retinopathy, which is the top cause of blindness in the working-age population. The disease can be treated and blindness prevented if it is detected early. Diabetics should be screened annually, but due to a shortage of ophthalmologists, high cost, and lack of access, only 25% of those who need it receive the screening. AEYE Health is a Tel Aviv and New York-based startup whose automated, AI-based diagnostic screening technology for retinal imaging is targeted initially at the large and growing diabetic retinopathy market. The company also plans to launch its platform that currently screens 12 additional eye indications, including glaucoma, AMD as well as systemic indications such as cardiovascular risk and dementia. Instead of needing an annual appointment with an ophthalmologist for screening, patients will be able to receive initial screening from their primary care physician or at other healthcare facilities including retail pharmacies. Only patients for which the AI system detects potential disease need to be referred to a specialist. A billable CPT code already exists for AI-based diabetic retinopathy screening, enabling physicians and other health care providers to generate additional revenue while protecting the vision of their patients. The details here are based on information received from, and verified solely by, the company.
